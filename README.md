DentMax is a well-established player in the market for intraoral digital sensors—used by dentists to take high-quality digital X-rays. 
These sensors vary by image clarity, software compatibility, ease of use, and post-sale support. For years, DentMax held a solid position, but recently, it has lost about 10% of market share to lower-priced competitors. 

To regain its edge, DentMax needs to better understand the dental market and tailor its offerings and messaging to meet dentists’ specific needs. 

The Challenge:
At DENTEXPO, the industry’s major trade show, DentMax collected rich data from dentists who visited their booth: 

RFID tracking data (e.g., time spent at the booth) 
Sales history* (e.g., purchase volume, product mix) 
Survey responses (e.g., preferences for product features and support) 

Our task was to analyze this data and identify segments within the market—groups of dentists who differ meaningfully in what they want and how they behave.
